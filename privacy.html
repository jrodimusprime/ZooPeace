Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026 · Zoo Peace Maker

This Privacy Policy explains how Zoo Peace Maker (https://jrodimusprime.github.io/ZooPeace/) handles information when you play the game.

1. Who we are

Zoo Peace Maker is a browser game published on GitHub Pages. For privacy questions, contact: jrodimusprime@users.noreply.github.com (or open an issue on the ZooPeace GitHub repository).

2. Information stored on your device

The game saves progress locally in your browser using localStorage. This may include:

  • Player name and avatar choice
  • Level, XP, gold, stats, and upgrades
  • Animal defeat counts and unlocks
  • Language preference and challenge progress

This data stays on your device unless you clear site data or reset progress in the game. We do not operate a game account server that receives this save data.

3. Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site uses Google AdSense to show advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites
  • Measure ad performance
  • Help prevent fraud and abuse

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to this website or other websites. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also visit www.aboutads.info/choices to opt out of personalized advertising from participating companies.

For more information, see Google’s Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites that use our services.

4. Analytics and logs

Hosting is provided by GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard technical logs (such as IP address, browser type, and request times) as described in GitHub’s privacy documentation.

5. Children

Zoo Peace Maker is a general-audience game. It is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this site in a way that concerns you, contact us and we will help address it where possible.

6. Your choices

  • Clear game saves via Reset Progress in the Stats tab, or by clearing browser site data
  • Block or limit cookies/ads via your browser or device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads using the links in section 3

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the game after changes means you accept the updated policy.
